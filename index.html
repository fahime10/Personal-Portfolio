JavaFX university assignment

This project involved the use of Java and utilises JavaFX to produce graphical outputs. The purpose of the app was to simulate a module chooser for students who needed to select final year modules. The app allows the student to sign in on the first page, then select modules for both first and second term (there is a limit based on credits). Students must also pick reserve modules on the third page. On the fourth page is the summary of the student's details, the first choice modules and reserve modules.
